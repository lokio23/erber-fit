# MASTER AESTHETIC WORKOUT PLAN (FORM GUIDE)

**Cycle continuously:**
Push → Pull → Knee-Friendly Legs/Core → Shoulders/V-Taper → repeat

Rest when your body feels overly fatigued.

---

## DAY 1 — PUSH
### Chest / Shoulders / Triceps

**1. Incline Dumbbell Press — 4×8–10**
Form tips:
- Bench at 30–45°
- Keep shoulder blades pulled back
- Lower dumbbells to upper chest
- Drive elbows slightly inward (not flared)

Avoid:
- Bouncing weight
- Arching lower back excessively

**2. Flat Dumbbell Press — 3×8–10**
Form tips:
- Feet planted firmly
- Shoulder blades locked back
- Slow 2–3 second lowering

Avoid:
- Letting elbows flare straight out

**3. Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press — 4×8–10**
Form tips:
- Dumbbells start ear level
- Press slightly inward at the top
- Keep core tight

Avoid:
- Excessive lower-back arch

**4. Dumbbell Lateral Raises — 4×12–15**
Form tips:
- Slight lean forward
- Raise slightly in front of body
- Stop at shoulder height
- Control lowering

Avoid:
- Swinging
- Shrugging traps

**5. Cable Lateral Raises — 3×12–15**
Form tips:
- Cable behind body
- Small bend in elbow
- Lift with side delt, not traps

**6. Assisted Dips — 3×10–12**
Form tips:
- Lean slightly forward
- Lower until elbows ~90°

Avoid:
- Dropping too deep (shoulder stress)

**7. Rope Tricep Pushdowns — 3×12**
Form tips:
- Elbows pinned to sides
- Spread rope at bottom

Avoid:
- Shoulders moving forward

**8. Overhead Tricep Extensions — 3×10**
Form tips:
- Keep elbows narrow
- Full stretch behind head

Avoid:
- Arching lower back

---

## DAY 2 — PULL
### Back / Rear Delts / Biceps

**1. Pullups or Lat Pulldown — 4×8–10**
Form tips:
- Pull elbows down toward ribs
- Slight lean back
- Chest up

Avoid:
- Pulling with arms only

**2. Seated Cable Row — 4×10**
Form tips:
- Neutral spine
- Pull to lower ribs
- Squeeze shoulder blades

Avoid:
- Leaning too far backward

**3. Single Arm Dumbbell Row — 3×10**
Form tips:
- Flat back
- Pull elbow toward hip

Avoid:
- Rotating torso

**4. Reverse Pec Deck (Rear Delts) — 4×12–15**
Form tips:
- Chest on pad
- Arms slightly bent
- Pull wide

Avoid:
- Squeezing traps

**5. Face Pulls — 3×15**
Form tips:
- Pull rope to upper face
- Elbows high
- Pause briefly

Avoid:
- Pulling too low

**6. Hammer Curls — 3×10–12**
Form tips:
- Neutral grip
- Elbows fixed

Avoid:
- Swinging weight

**7. Incline Dumbbell Curls — 3×10**
Form tips:
- Full stretch at bottom
- Curl slowly

Avoid:
- Lifting elbows forward

**8. Reverse Curls — 3×12**
Form tips:
- Overhand grip
- Slow negatives

This builds forearms and arm vascularity.

---

## DAY 3 — KNEE-FRIENDLY LEGS + CORE
### Focus on glutes and hamstrings to protect knees.

**1. Romanian Deadlifts — 4×8–10**
Form tips:
- Hinge hips back
- Slight knee bend
- Keep bar close to legs

Avoid:
- Rounding lower back

**2. Hip Thrusts or Glute Bridges — 4×10**
Form tips:
- Chin tucked
- Drive through heels
- Squeeze glutes at top

Avoid:
- Hyperextending lower back

**3. Seated or Lying Hamstring Curl — 3×12**
Form tips:
- Slow negatives
- Full contraction

Avoid:
- Jerking weight

**4. Step-Ups — 3×10 each leg**
Form tips:
- Low box height
- Push through heel

Avoid:
- Pushing off back foot

**5. Leg Press (Feet High) — 3×10**
Form tips:
- Feet high on platform
- Control descent
- Knees track with toes

Avoid:
- Locking knees

**6. Standing Calf Raises — 4×15**
Form tips:
- Full stretch at bottom
- Pause at top

Avoid:
- Bouncing

**7. Hanging Leg Raises — 4×12–15**
Form tips:
- Raise legs using abs
- Slow control

Avoid:
- Swinging

**8. Cable Crunch — 3×15**
Form tips:
- Curl spine downward
- Use abs, not hips

---

## DAY 4 — SHOULDERS + V-TAPER
### This is your aesthetic physique builder.

**1. Seated Shoulder Press — 4×8**
Focus on controlled movement.

**2. Dumbbell Lateral Raises — 5×15**
Keep weight moderate for perfect form.

**3. Cable Lateral Raises — 4×12**
Constant tension = bigger side delts.

**4. Rear Delt Fly — 4×15**
Keep arms wide.

**5. Wide Grip Lat Pulldown — 4×10**
Pull elbows outward and down.

**6. Straight Arm Pulldown — 3×12**
Focus on lat contraction, not arms.

**7. Incline Dumbbell Press — 3×10**
Upper chest builder.

**8. Hanging Leg Raises — 3×15**

**9. Ab Wheel Rollouts — 3×10**
Keep core tight.

Avoid:
- Letting hips drop

## GENERAL INJURY PREVENTION RULES

**Warm up 5–10 minutes before lifting.**

Best warmup:
- Incline walk
- Band shoulder work
- Glute activation

### Rep Tempo (important)
Use controlled tempo: **2 seconds lifting, 2–3 seconds lowering**

Most injuries happen when people rush reps.

### Stop 1–2 reps before failure
Training to failure constantly leads to fatigue and joint strain.

---

## PROGRESSION

Each week aim to:
- Increase weight slightly **OR**
- Increase reps

Small progress compounds over time.

---

## IDEAL CARDIO

**After workouts:**
Incline treadmill walk 10–20 minutes, 10–15% incline

This is one of the best fat-loss tools while maintaining muscle.
